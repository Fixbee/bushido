About Us

Bushido Academy Of Martial Arts & Fitness Hub is formed out of sheer passion for Karate. At a time when Karate is losing its values and turning into a mere Business, we strive for excellence in the field of karate as well as mental and physical fitness of our students. we encourage and guide our next generation to instill themselves with the seven virtues of Bushido with the objective of making a better individual.
